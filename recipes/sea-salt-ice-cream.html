Sea Salt Ice Cream

Description

Blue ice cream you eat with your homies after working for a nefarious organization in order to feel like a person.

Ingredients

  • Be in a "zombie" like state due to having been born, quite literally, yesterday
  • 1 1/2 Cup of milk
  • 1 1/2 Cup of cream
  • 2 Friends, because if you don't, who else will you eat ice cream with?
  • 1 Tall ass clock tower in which to sit atop
  • 3 Tbsp of Cornstarch
  • 3/4 Cup of sugar
  • 1 Tsp of sea salt
  • Trauma stemming from not knowing who you are
  • Teal gel-based food coloring

Steps

  1. Mix together 1 ½ cups each of high quality milk and cream.
  2. Realize you're a clone of somebody else.
  3. Spiral.
  4. Combine 3 Tbsp cornstarch in about 1 cup of milk and cream mixture until it is dissolved and no lumps remain.
  5. Bring the remaining milk and cream mixture close to a simmer on the stovetop.
  6. Then, ¾ cup sugar is added directly to the milk and cream on the stovetop until it dissolves over medium-low heat until just simmering.
  7. At this point you will add the cornstarch mixture until combined. Let that all cook for about 1 minute.
  8. Add one heaping tsp of sea salt to custard on stovetop as it finishes thickening. Then strain into a heat-proof bowl. Add some gel-based teal food coloring.
  9. Make sure not to kill your friend who is slowly turning into the person you're a clone of.
  10. Into an ice cream machine it goes for about 25 minutes.
  11. Enjoy! :)
